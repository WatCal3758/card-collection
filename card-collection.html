Ben Roethlisberger

  • 2x Super Bowl Champion
  • 6x Pro Bowl Member
  • 2014 NFL Passing Yards Co-Leader
  • 2004 NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year
  • MAC Offensive Player of the Year
QB - Pittsburgh Steelers

Coming out of the University of Miami in Ohio, Roethlisberger was drafted with the 11th pick in the 1st round by the Pittsburgh Steelers. He has played with the Steelers since he was drafted in 2004, accumulating over 52,000 passing yards and average a passer rating of 94.0. This passer rating ranks him as 9th all time in the NFL.

Antonio Brown

  • 4x First-team All-Pro
  • 6x Pro Bowl Member
  • 2x NFL Receiving Yards Leader
  • 2x NFL Receptions Leader
  • 2x First-team All-American
WR - Pittsburgh Steelers

Coming out of Centeral Michigan University, Brown was drafted with the 195th pick in the 6th round in 2010 by the Pittsburgh Steelers. A sixth round pick has not gained more receptions or yards than Antonio Brown. In 2018, Brown surpassed the 10,000 career recieving yards mark, an incredible feat.

Troy Polamalu

  • 2x Super Bowl Champion
  • 8x Pro Bowl Member
  • 4x First-team All-Pro
  • 2010 NFL Defensive Player of the Year
  • NFL 2000s All-Decade Team
ILB - Pittsburgh Steelers

Coming out of the University of Southern California, Polamalu was drafted with the 16th overall pick in the 1st round by the Pittsburgh Steelers, a team he played for 11 years for. Over his entire career he accumulated 770 tackles and 12 sacks. Polamalu's NFL career ended in 2014 when he retired.
